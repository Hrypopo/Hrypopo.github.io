Spec

The one-breath promise.

A teaser doesn't explain the city. It makes you need to see it. Ninety seconds: silence, a discovery, a descent, a montage of the impossible, a threat — then the title, and a door left open.

The board · 16 shots

Shot by shot.

00:00SH 01

Pure black. Held three full seconds. Then a low sound felt before heard.

SoundSilence → a sub-bass hum rising from below the frame.
Text— nothing yet —
00:03SH 02

A dead, cracked plain under a bruise-coloured sky. Wind. Dust. The end of somewhere.

SoundWind. The hum persists underneath, wrong for an empty place.
VO“They asked me why I'd build in the dark.”
00:09SH 03

A single shaft of warm gold light punches up out of the earth. The only light for a hundred miles.

SoundA tone blooms under the hum — hopeful, alien.
VO“So I'd have a reason to bring my own.”
00:15SH 04

Camera drops into the shaft — surface falls away above, gold light rushing past. We're going down.

SoundWhoosh of descent; hum climbs in pitch.
TextEveryone builds up.
00:22SH 05

Reveal: stratum after stratum of a city carved downward, terraces glowing, tiny figures moving. Vast.

SoundThe hum resolves into a warm chord. First sign of life.
TextHe built down.
00:30SH 06

The heart: the reactor core behind glass, breathing cyan, a contained sun. Faces lit in awe.

SoundDeep resonant pulse — the city's heartbeat.
VO“Down here, there's no dark. We brought our own light.”
00:38SH 07

MONTAGE (fast): a maglev pod tears down the spine; vertical farms flush green; data vaults glow.

SoundRhythmic percussive build; each cut lands on a beat.
TextPower. Water. Food. Air.
00:48SH 08

Surface, boardroom glass, cold daylight. A powerful figure watches the glowing portal on a screen, unsmiling.

SoundMusic drops out to a single ominous note.
VO“A city they can't tax. Can't zone. Can't switch off.”
00:56SH 09

Quick cuts of conflict: a hearing, a hand on a red-lined gauge, workers massing on the maglev spine.

SoundTension stabs; the hum turns anxious, detuned.
TextThey will try to bury it.
01:04SH 10

Hard cut to near-black — only the core's cyan glow, pulsing once, twice, like a held breath.

SoundEverything drops to silence but the heartbeat.
VO“Let them try.”
01:10SH 11

The whole geode floods with light top-to-bottom at once — the money shot. Gold and cyan, every stratum alive.

SoundChoral swell explodes; the full theme lands.
TextThe future isn't up there.
