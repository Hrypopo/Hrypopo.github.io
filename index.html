I'm Lehlohonolo Goodwill Nchefu, a solo founder operating under the name Chevza — building from South Africa with a bias toward privacy, local-first architecture, and tools that work even when the internet doesn't.

FileMayor started as a personal itch: I needed a file organiser that my AI could actually drive, not just suggest. Two years later it's a full CLI + Desktop + PWA ecosystem with its own security doctrine, 25,000+ installs, and a growing weekly install rate.

I care about distribution. I care about honesty in software. And I'm convinced the most interesting tools will be built at the intersection of AI and local-first computing.
