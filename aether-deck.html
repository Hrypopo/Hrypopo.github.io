The problem

Every megacity solves the same three problems badly.

  • 01Energy — imported, carbon-heavy, price-volatile.
  • 02Land — finite, speculative, endlessly sprawling.
  • 03Climate — the building fights the weather every single day.

We've optimised the surface for a century. The variable no one questions is direction.

The insight

Build down.

Twenty metres down, the temperature is stable, the energy is free, and the land is infinite.

What it is

A city built like a geode.

A near-invisible surface cap over nine descending strata — residences, commercial tunnels, farms, data, and a reactor at the heart. Hard shell, luminous core.

L0 · surface cap L1–3 · terraces L4–8 · commerce · farms · data L9 · reactor core

System 01 · Energy

The waste heat is the whole point.

One small modular reactor delivers constant baseload. Its waste heat — normally dumped — feeds climate, agritech, desalination, and compute.
