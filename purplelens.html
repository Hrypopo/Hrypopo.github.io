Behind the lens

PurpleLens is a one-person studio that chases warm, honest light — in faces, in products, in the spaces between people.

I'm Lehlohonolo 'Chevza' Nchefu, a maker and photographer based in Johannesburg. PurpleLens grew out of a simple belief: a photograph should feel like the moment did — unhurried, true, and a little bit golden.

I shoot portraits, brand & commercial work, weddings, and a personal line of fine-art prints. Whether it's a founder's headshot or a couple's first dance, the approach is the same — patience, warmth, and an eye for the frame everyone else walks past.
