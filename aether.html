The executive thesis

We have spent a century building upward and outward. AETHER is the case for building down — where the temperature is stable, the energy is free, and the land is infinite.

This is the foundational DNA: the architectural skeleton that holds the entire concept together. It proves the logic, the physics, and the economics work before a single tunnel is bored.

A small modular reactor (SMR) supplies the city with constant baseload power and, critically, waste heat — which becomes the input for district climate control and year-round agritech. Autonomous maglev moves people and freight on a vectored routing mesh with no surface traffic. Tokenised commercial tunnels turn real estate into a programmable, liquid asset class that funds the build. And the city exports what it makes best — clean compute, food, and intellectual property.

If you handed this to a builder who thinks in centuries, this is the manifesto they would read on the flight.

The whitepaper · core systems

Four systems, one organism.

The deep verticals — the physics and the spreadsheets behind the render. Figures below are concept-stage and to be replaced with the production model.

01 · EnergySMR

The thermal core

A small modular reactor delivers constant baseload power. Its waste heat is not lost — it is piped as the primary input for climate control, agritech, and desalination.

  • Output: ~300 MWe baseload
  • Waste heat captured: ≈ 60% reused
  • Thermal dissipation: closed-loop to strata
  • Footprint: sub-surface, sealed
02 · TransitMAGLEV

Autonomous arteries

People and freight ride a vectored maglev mesh. Routing is fully autonomous — no surface traffic, no signals, just continuous flow solved as a real-time vector problem.

  • Topology: helical + radial mesh
  • Routing: autonomous vector solver
  • Surface roads required: zero
  • Freight + transit on one spine
03 · StructureTUNNELS

Subterranean shell

The city is bored, not built. Engineered tunnel rings carry the structural load while keeping the interior at a stable temperature the surface can never offer.

  • Strata: 9 load-bearing rings
  • Interior climate: 21°C constant
  • Load model: ring-distributed
  • Daylight: fibre-optic sun pipes
04 · EconomyTOKENS

Programmable tunnels

Commercial tunnels are issued as on-chain containers — fractional, tradable, and revenue-bearing. Real estate becomes a liquid asset class that funds the build itself.

  • Unit: tokenised container
  • Micro-transactions on usage
  • Liquidity: fractional + tradable
  • Funds capex from day one
05 · OutputEXPORTS

What the city sells

AETHER is a net exporter. Reactor-cheap power makes its compute and food globally competitive; its designs become licensable IP for the next city.

  • Agritech: year-round yield
  • Compute: liquid-cooled, low-cost
  • IP: the blueprint, licensed
  • Clean power: surplus to grid
06 · ResilienceSEALED

Built to outlast

Underground means weatherproof, blast-tolerant, and thermally efficient by default. The city is engineered for a thousand-year horizon, not a quarterly one.

  • Surface weather: irrelevant
  • Thermal mass: vast, stable
  • Redundancy: ring isolation
  • Horizon: 1,000 years
